About this project

The aim of this website is to promote mindful city walks as a method that has the power to inspire and encourage people to find more headspace in their everyday lives. In choosing the now of the situations we find ourselves in while walking through the cities, in mindfully observing our surroundings and discovering the emotional impact of urban space lies the potential of reaching a meditative state of mind and with that creating more space in our heads.
